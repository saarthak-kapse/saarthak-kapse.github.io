Biography

I’m a 3rd year PhD candidate at Stony Brook University, advised by Prof. Prateek Prasanna. My broad interests lie in Computer Vision and its applications in computational pathology, with a specific focus on utilizing histopathology priors. I earned my Bachelor's degree in Electrical Engineering from IIT Bombay, India, in 2020, where I had the opportunity to work under the guidance of Prof. Amit Sethi. During the summer of 2019, I interned at the Philips Innovation Campus in Bangalore, India, contributing to their research team.
